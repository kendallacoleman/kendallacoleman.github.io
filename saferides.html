Saferides Project

SafeRides is an entirely student-run FEB organization that offers free and safe rides home to JMU students on Friday and Saturday nights from 10PM-3AM.


I am currently working on a team to develop a web application for Saferides members to use while working an "Ops" night. This web application will help those working at Saferides HQ to be able to quickly and effectively communicate with Saferides drivers and navigators.


In this project, I aim to enhance my skills in front-end development. Stay tuned to this page for updates through development!
